How to Navigate the Platform

Our platform is designed to be intuitive. Use the navigation bar to explore destinations, find upcoming events, or view your personalized itineraries on the interactive map.

Creating Itineraries

Go to the "Itineraries" section to start planning your trip. You can add destinations, set dates, and organize your daily activities easily.

Booking Events

Discover local festivals, workshops, and tours in the "Events" section. Simply select an event and follow the booking process to secure your spot.

Managing Reservations

Access your profile to view and manage all your active reservations, past trips, and saved destinations.
